Drug and Alcohol Abuse (DAA) Facilities Policy Review

A. DAA REQUIREMENTS/RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. DAA facilities are required to be either tax exempt or certified by the state agency responsible for the treatment of drug addicts and alcoholics (receiving funding under PART B of Title XIX, eligible to receive funding, or operating to provide treatment and rehabilitation of drug addicts and/or alcoholics.
  2. DAA facilities are required to be certified by the FNS as an authorized retailer to accept the EBT card.
  3. DAA Facility Rep/AR must complete face-to-face interview. Interviews by telephone are not allowed. The AR must apply in person, be an employee at the facility, have verification of employment at the facility, and have verification that he/she is designated to apply.
  4. THE DAA FACILITIES MUST PROVIDE PROOF OF LICENSURE/CERTIFICATION FOR EVERY APPLICATION. THIS MUST BE SCANNED INTO ECF AT APPLICATION.

B. THE CLIENT
Clients who are regularly participating in authorized drug or alcohol treatment and rehabilitation programs on a residential basis may apply to receive SNAP benefits. THE AR MUST APPLY FOR THE CLIENT. THE CLIENT MAY NOT APPLY WHILE RESIDING IN A TREATMENT FACILITY.


C. WORKER PORTAL
The application will be conducted and verified in the same manner as all other SNAP applications.

  1. Household size will be 1 unless the resident's children are also residing in the treatment facility with him. In this case, the children will also be included in the household.
  2. Living Arrangement Screen:
    1. Living Arrangement Start Date must be date of admission into facility.
    2. Living Arrangement Type: Drug Addiction and Alcohol Treatment Centers
    3. Living Arrangement Verification: usually will be verified by ws from facility/AR.
    4. Living Arrangement Organization/Provider Box:
      1. From date: must be date of admission
      2. Is this an ineligible facility? Select "NO" if facility is licenced and authorized to apply for SNAP
      3. Organization/Provider:Select facility from drop down list
      4. Has 50% meals:For this question, ask the AR if client is going to receive any meals covered by the facility. Or is client responsible for paying for all of his/her own meals? Select NO if client has to pay for all his/her own meals.
    5. CLICK UPDATE.
  3. Drug Felonies: Be sure to ask this question on the non-financial information screen. If client has been convicted of a felony, the facility will usually know.
  4. Chemical Dependence Assessment/Program Completion: If the client has been convicted of a felony, the AR and Facility will need to provide participation verification. Upon completion, they will need to supply completion letter.
Note: For more information on DAA policy see the full DCBS Family Support SNAP Manual.
